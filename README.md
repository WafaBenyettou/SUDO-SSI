### 🎙️ Podcasts
Podcasts can be an excellent resource for staying updated and deepening your understanding of cybersecurity. Here are some highly recommended podcasts in the field:
![image](https://github.com/WafaBenyettou/SUDO-SSI/assets/83190429/d9513f2d-a91d-4aeb-aec9-6ba151fe7845)

#### 🎧 1. Darknet Diaries
**Host:** Jack Rhysider

**Description:** Darknet Diaries dives into true stories from the dark side of the internet. The host interviews hackers, security experts, and victims of cybercrime, providing listeners with fascinating and often chilling insights into the world of cybersecurity.

**Link:** [Darknet Diaries](https://darknetdiaries.com/)

#### 🎧 2. SecurityNow
**Hosts:** Steve Gibson and Leo Laporte

**Description:** SecurityNow covers a wide range of security topics, including the latest vulnerabilities, exploits, and patches. The hosts break down complex concepts into understandable discussions, making it a great resource for both beginners and seasoned professionals.

**Link:** [SecurityNow](https://twit.tv/shows/security-now)

#### 🎧 3. CyberWire Daily
**Host:** Dave Bittner

**Description:** CyberWire Daily provides a quick, daily briefing on the latest cybersecurity news. It covers industry updates, significant cyber incidents, and expert analysis, keeping listeners informed about current trends and threats.

**Link:** [CyberWire Daily](https://thecyberwire.com/podcasts/daily-podcast)

#### 🎧 4. Malicious Life
**Host:** Ran Levi

**Description:** Malicious Life explores the history of cybersecurity and cybercrime. Through storytelling and expert interviews, the podcast delves into significant events and figures that have shaped the cybersecurity landscape.

**Link:** [Malicious Life](https://malicious.life/)

#### 🎧 5. Risky Business
**Host:** Patrick Gray

**Description:** Risky Business offers news and in-depth analysis on the latest in cybersecurity. It features interviews with industry experts and covers topics ranging from cyber policy to new security technologies.

**Link:** [Risky Business](https://risky.biz/)

#### 🎧 6. Hacking Humans
**Hosts:** Dave Bittner and Joe Carrigan

**Description:** Hacking Humans focuses on social engineering attacks and human vulnerabilities in cybersecurity. The hosts discuss recent scams, phishing attacks, and techniques used by attackers to exploit human psychology.

**Link:** [Hacking Humans](https://thecyberwire.com/podcasts/hacking-humans)

#### 🎧 7. Smashing Security
**Hosts:** Graham Cluley and Carole Theriault

**Description:** Smashing Security blends humor with serious discussions on cybersecurity news and trends. The hosts and their guests talk about the latest threats, hacks, and practical advice for staying safe online.

**Link:** [Smashing Security](https://www.smashingsecurity.com/)

#### 🎧 8. Unsupervised Learning
**Host:** Daniel Miessler

**Description:** Unsupervised Learning offers a blend of cybersecurity, technology, and society. The host provides weekly summaries of the most important security news and insights into how technology is shaping the world.

**Link:** [Unsupervised Learning](https://danielmiessler.com/podcast/)

### 🕹️ CTF Platforms
Sharpen your cybersecurity skills with these CTF platforms:

![image](https://github.com/WafaBenyettou/SUDO-SSI/assets/83190429/e387e238-b373-4a43-8382-db25ea640e7c)


#### 🎮 1. Hack The Box (HTB)
**Description:** A popular platform with a wide range of challenges, including active machines and retired ones for various skill levels.

**Link:** [Hack The Box](https://www.hackthebox.eu/)

#### 🎮 2. TryHackMe
**Description:** Offers guided, hands-on learning paths and challenges that range from beginner to advanced.

**Link:** [TryHackMe](https://tryhackme.com/)

#### 🎮 3. OverTheWire
**Description:** Known for its series of war games that help users learn and practice security concepts.

**Link:** [OverTheWire](https://overthewire.org/wargames/)

#### 🎮 4. Root-Me
**Description:** Provides a variety of challenges in different categories, including web, network, and crypto.

**Link:** [Root-Me](https://www.root-me.org/)

#### 🎮 5. PicoCTF
**Description:** Aimed at beginners and students, PicoCTF offers a variety of fun and educational challenges.

**Link:** [PicoCTF](https://picoctf.org/)

#### 🎮 6. CTFtime
**Description:** A comprehensive calendar of upcoming CTF competitions, rankings, and resources.

**Link:** [CTFtime](https://ctftime.org/)

#### 🎮 7. Hacker101 CTF
**Description:** Hosted by HackerOne, this platform offers CTF challenges that align with real-world bug bounty hunting.

**Link:** [Hacker101 CTF](https://ctf.hacker101.com/)

#### 🎮 8. HackThisSite
**Description:** An interactive and educational platform offering a wide range of hacking challenges.

**Link:** [HackThisSite](https://www.hackthissite.org/)

#### 🎮 9. VulnHub
**Description:** Provides downloadable virtual machines that contain various vulnerabilities to exploit.

**Link:** [VulnHub](https://www.vulnhub.com/)

#### 🎮 10. RingZer0 Team
**Description:** A CTF platform with numerous challenges in various categories, including reversing, exploitation, and more.

**Link:** [RingZer0 Team](https://ringzer0ctf.com/)
